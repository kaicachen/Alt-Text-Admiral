Who is Alt-Text Admiral?

Alt-Text Admiral is a team of young, passionate developers from The University of Kansas.


Why did we make Alt-Text Admiral?

This project is the Capstone of our time spent studying Computer Science. We have worked hard to learn many things about working with computers and on the internet, but one of the most important things we have learned is the importance of community and accessibility. We built this tool to help make the internet a more accessible place for everyone.
